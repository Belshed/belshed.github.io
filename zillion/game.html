Guardians of Valhalla

Under the blue northern sky there is a golden city, with transparent gates, and the stars shining above. This is Valhalla, the home of gods and heroes, filled with untold riches. It is protected by animal-looking spirits of the nature.

About game

The Guardians of Valhalla is a classic 3x5 reels game, which allows a player transfer to the Norse mythology world and try to get the access the inhuman fortunes of Vallhalla. ( In case spirits and gods will find you worthy of course).

Mythical beasts

The player is awaited by the spirits-animals from northern mythology - the mighty riding boar Gullinbursti, which belonged to the god Freyr, the majestic deer Eikturnir. And the presence of Hugin and Munin the ravens - is clearly a hint that the All-Father himself leads the guards.

Supreme Guard: Allfather Odin - Scatter

Odin, or Wotan, is the eldest not only among the guards, but among all the gods of Asgard. He exchanged his eye for the knowledge of the runes, and with their help he is able to turn any situation to his advantage. To meet him several times is already a great success, but if he appears to you more than three times - it will start free spins. Fate itself will bend to his will, and play along with you and shower you with gold.

Valkyrie - Expanding Wild

Where Odin walks, his loyal Valkyries, war maidens, patrons of battle and good luck follow him. They are known by the fact that they see off the fallen soldiers, but few people know how they try to ensure that as many soldiers as possible survive. If the Valkyrie finds even the slightest chance to help the player, she will try her best. And her symbol will stretch over three slots of one reel.
